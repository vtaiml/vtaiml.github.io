about

We're a beginner-friendly club focused on helping disseminate info about machine learning. Our club primarily consists of theory lectures/workshops. We go over the behind-the-hood of different ML architectures, show examples of real use cases, and discuss current events in AI.

If you're a student and you want to get in touch, the best way is via Discord. You can also reach out to us via email at vtaimclub@gmail.com.
